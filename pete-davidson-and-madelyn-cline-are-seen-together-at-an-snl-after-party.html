Pete Davidson and Madelyn Cline Are Seen Together at an SNL After-Party

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Pete Davidson and Madelyn Cline may have just gone public with their rumored romance.

Last night, the comedian and the actor were seen entering the after-party following Davidson’s stint hosting Saturday Night Live’s first episode of its 49th season. The sighting comes a month after reports that the pair had begun dating following their recent breakups, including Davidson’s split with actor Chase Sui Wonders in August.

In photos taken captured outside their party, Davidson and Cline keep their distance while arriving at Catch Steak alongside friend and actor Sofia Hublitz. Cline wore an all-black outfit comprised of a semi-sheer black turtleneck top, baggy pants, and sneakers. Her array of gold jewelry included layered necklaces, a TAG Heuer watch, and a green square ring. Meanwhile, Davidson kept a low profile under a gray baseball cap, while dressed in a green, brown, and black tracksuit and tan boots.

Gotham//Getty Images

A source previously told Us Weekly that the pair have “grown close” over several weeks of dating and that they wanted to “keep things under wraps” for as long as possible.

“[They] had hoped to remain low-key for a while, especially because Madelyn is a really private person,” the insider said. “But they’ve spent a lot of time together and have built a strong bond in a short period of time.”

Cline recently ended her yearlong relationship with singer Jackson Guthy, and also previously dated her Outer Banks costar Chase Stokes. Davidson’s romantic history is often a subject of public attention. Aside from Wonders, he has dated Kim Kardashian, Phoebe Dynevor, Ariana Grande, and Cassie David.
