Shoppers Walk 20 Miles in the On-Sale Adidas Cloudfoam Sneakers

Yeah, that, too. So when I saw one of the most well-known women in media pose in Adidas’ Cloudfoam Pure 2.0 running shoes in an Instagram post, I had to do a double take. Did she really want a pair of kicks that are on sale right now? Yes, yes she did — and you will, too.

Made with a flexible, stretchy upper, super cushioned midsole, and resilient bottom, they’re ideal for a plethora of activities, such as running around the block, heading to work, and simply just living in them. Best of all, the iconic white and black color option is currently $30 off the original $75 price tag. 

One Amazon shopper called the Adidas Cloudfoam Pure 2.0 sneaker “everything [they] were looking for in a shoe” and said it’s “incredibly comfortable.” Another walked “nearly 20 miles” in these kicks and “did not have one single ache or pain,” adding that they’re “absolutely sold” on and “love” the style.  

I can attest to the Adidas’ Cloudfoam Pure 2.0 running shoe’s qualities, as I have my own all-black pair. I’ve worn them cycling, hiking, out to eat, and everything in between. They’re the do-it-all sneaker you didn’t know you needed. And not to be dramatic, but it will most likely become your new favorite sneaker.
