The webtoon is set in a world where people with special abilities are divided into two groups: villains, who are driven by their evil desires, and psykers, who pursue justice and work against villains.

Cassian was a very powerful psyker with a good heart, but he was killed by his colleagues, who accused him of murdering his friend.
