Lil Durk &quot;Too Late&quot; Video

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Combining the visual diary concept to the music video isn't anything new but Lil Durk's "Too Late" makes it a fascinating watch nonetheless. In his latest video, the camera follows the rapper as he makes his promotional tour across the country, spitting his bars, visiting strip clubs and fashion shows, and sipping on lean with his crew. 

The video starts out in New York City, where Durk goes on Sway’s radio show. He then chills at VIBE’s offices and poses for a promotional photo shoot before flying out to Los Angeles to get interviews on radio there. The video edit is as frenetic as Durk's flow. It makes you want to watch it again. The track is off the Chicago native's latest mixtape They Forgot.
