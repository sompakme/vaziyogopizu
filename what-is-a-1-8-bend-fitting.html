What is a 1/8 Bend fitting?

September 2024 · 5 minute read

Answer

DWV PVC fittings feature what are known as 1/8 bends, which are similar to a 45-degree elbow. This curve has HUB ends, which means that the pipe slides into each end of the bend. Size of 1-1/2″ DWV PVC pipe. Constructed to be used in drain, garbage, and vent applications.

One-eighth bend elbow is used to join two pipes at a 45-degree angle. Hub ends are used to join male unthreaded pipes or fittings together. PVC is used for fittings because of its corrosion resistance, high strength, and less weight than metal fittings.
