Lalah Hathaway's musical journey began with her self-titled debut album in 1990, which featured the hit single "Heaven Knows." Building on her debut, Hathaway released albums highlighting her evolving artistry. Hathaway's ability to blend different genres while maintaining her unique sound has been a key factor in her sustained success.
