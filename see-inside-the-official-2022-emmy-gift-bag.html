See Inside the Official 2022 Emmy Gift Bag

August 2024 · 3 minute read

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

There are no losers when it comes to awards season gifting in Hollywood, and this year’s official Emmys swag bag will treat the presenters, nominees and winners to freebies valued at a total of $10,000 and up ranging from luxurious vacations in Montana and Turkey to Aveeno skincare and more.

Backstage at the Microsoft Theater (where the Emmys will air live Monday, Sept. 12), invited guests can swing by Backstage Creations’ Official Emmy Giving Suite and get scanned by sponsor Hasbro Selfie Series for a custom action figure made in their image, sip on functional mocktails by Mude (another main event sponsor) and non-alcoholic So.Beer and “shop” for custom jewelry by Erin Flynn and vegan fur robes by SpiritHoods. Those in need of a post-ceremony getaway can book decadent three-night stays at the Four Seasons Nevis in the West Indies (from $673 nightly); the Clark Fork River-adjacent River View Ranch Montana (suites from $2,800), where all-inclusive experiences include horseback trail rides, private shooting ranges and stand-up paddle board yoga; and Museum Hotel Cappadocia Turkey (rooms from $500), a “living museum” boutique hotel transformed from ancient cave ruins that includes a historic pool and works of art.

Attendees also go home with Ricardo Beverly Hills luggage packed with skin care by Aveeno and Circle 5; Nutrol hair growth supplements; the Golden Girls cookbook; the Lifetime Lineup card game; Comvita certified UMF Manuka honey; a sampler of Vahdam teas; author Mark Bryan’s The Artist’s Way at Work: Riding the Dragon; personalized stationery by Crane; and decadent sweets by North Carolina-bases Dr. Monkey’s Ridiculously Good Caramels.

The suitcase also includes home spa essentials such as an LED facial device by Nu Skin, bath salts by Crystal Hill Organics, CBD tinctures by Austin-based Elevated Wellness and the Theracane deep pressure massager.
