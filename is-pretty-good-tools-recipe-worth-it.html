What is the ultimate pocket stuffing in Animal Crossing?

The Ultimate Pocket Stuffing is an item in Animal Crossing: New Horizons. When used, it increases the player’s pocket storage to 40, the maximum in New Horizons. This item cannot be placed in the player’s storage. The Ultimate Pocket Stuffing can be obtained from Nook Stop for 8,000 Nook Miles.

What’s the pretty good tools recipes?

The Pretty Good Tools Recipes in Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a recipe book that gives access to upgraded forms of the flimsy tools. These tools take the appearance of the default tools from previous Animal Crossing titles.

Why can’t I get the axe in Animal Crossing?

To unlock the axe recipe – basically the document required to craft an axe – you’ll need to speak to Tom Nook in the Resident Services tent. Give him fish or bugs (the ratio doesn’t matter) and he’ll give you the recipe for a flimsy axe, which is the weakest axe in the game.
