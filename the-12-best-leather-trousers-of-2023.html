The 12 best leather trousers of 2023

July 2024 · 4 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Getty Images/Gotham

Joseph

Joseph has long been known for creating some of the best leather trousers in the business. This particular style, the Den is backed onto stretch-cotton to give it a flexible, contour-hugging fit that always feels comfortable. Practical points aside, they’re also cut to a timeless straight-leg shape that’ll look great with pretty much anything.

Bottega Veneta

Bottega Veneta

Possibly the most anticipated piece of the entire season, Bottega Veneta’s trompe l'oeil trousers have finally arrived. A seamless showcase of the brand’s unrivalled craftsmanship, they look just like a pair of love-worn denim jeans, but look a little closer to see they’re actually made from ultra-supple leather. Invest now, treasure forever.

Agolde

Agolde

Now 30% Off

Those who want the look of genuine leather with the ease of extra stretch will love Agolde's Pinch Waist design. They're made from a low-waste blend of 50 per cent repurposed leather and 50 per cent stretch-infused synthetic fibres, so feel just like your favourite pair of jeans. Cut to sit snugly on the waist and fall in straight legs, they'll prove just as versatile, too.

Massimo Dutti

Massimo Dutti

Now 57% Off

With a slim waistband and minimalist finishing, Massimo Dutti's supple trousers are a refined option for smarter occasions. The rich khaki hue will work especially well with cream silk blouses and cuddly beige knits.

Wandler

Wandler

Now 50% Off

Coloured leather is big news for AW23, but Wandler’s tapered-leg trousers are more than a one-season wonder. The baby-pink hue will make a collection of camel and beige separates feel fresh and new: try them with a sandy cashmere jumper and see.

Arket

High-street leather can be hit and miss, but Arket gets it right every time. The well-cut, wide-leg silhouette of these supple trousers will hang beautifully, while the rich burgundy hue feels on-point for the winter.

Dorothee Schumacher

Dorothee Schumacher

Now 30% Off

When cut from light and fluid leather, a wide-leg silhouette looks especially striking. Dorothee Schumacher has got the balance just right in this stellar pair.

Cos

Crafted to last, Cos' leather trousers sit high on your natural waist, before falling in slim legs to softly flared hems. Ending neatly on the ankles, they'll look especially chic with a pair of the new season's ballet flats.

Walter Baker

Walter Baker

Now 59% Off

Soft shades of brown are never really out of fashion, but this season we’re seeing sepia tones everywhere. These leather trousers are the best way to indulge: hitting just above the ankle, they’ll work with flats and heels alike.

8 By Yoox

8 By Yoox

Now 58% Off

Past-season e-tailer Yoox creates some stellar wardrobe essentials in its own-label line, 8. These leather trousers are cut to a wide-leg silhouette that feels instantly chic, and finished with minimal detailing to ensure total versatility.

Muubaa

Muubaa

Now 62% Off

In a glossy patent finish, these trousers from leather specialists Muubaa will add a glamorous edge to evening looks. Team them with a white tank top by day, then elevate with fluid cream silk after hours.

Frame

Frame

Now 63% Off

Nonchalant yet elegant, the cut of Frame’s tapered trousers is just right (as you'd rightly expect from a brand famed for its ultra-flattering jeans). They sit softly on the natural waist before falling into wide legs that taper gently towards the ankle.

Alexander McQueen

Alexander McQueen

A star piece from the AW23 runway, these sublime leather trousers showcase Sarah Burton's razor-sharp tailoring to perfection. They're designed to sit high on the waist, before flaring from sharp knife pleats into wide, dramatic legs. Wear them as the designer intended: with a point-collar shirt and leg-lengthening heels.

& Other Stories

& Other Stories

Now 20% Off

From & Other Stories' elevated Savoir collection, these slick leather trousers are tailored to a slim, tapered silhouette. They're cut without a bulky waistband for a cool Nineties feel, and feature silver zips at the ankles that lend just a touch of biker edge.

Joseph

Joseph

Now 60% Off

The elasticated waistband of Joseph's Ashbridge trousers creates a louche, sporty silhouette (and ensures maximum comfort). Falling into wide legs, this pair is perfect for off-duty days – but a platform heel will take them into evening territory, too.
