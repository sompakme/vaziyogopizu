The Memphis Tigers: March Madness Veterans (But Not Quite Champions...Yet)

The Memphis Tigers are a force to be reckoned with in college basketball. They've got a winning tradition, a rabid fanbase (Tiger Nation, anyone?), and a mascot that's way cooler than a giant blue wildcat (sorry, Kentucky). But one burning question remains: just how far have these Tigers clawed their way in the NCAA Tournament? Buckle up, because we're about to dive into the Memphis Final Four Fun Facts!

How Many Final Fours Have the Memphis Tigers Been To?

Drumroll, please... Memphis has roared into the Final Four a very respectable three times! That's right, three trips to the college basketball promised land. Here's a quick breakdown:

  • 1973: The Pioneers - Back in the day when leisure suits were hot and disco was about to explode, the Memphis Tigers, then known as the University of Memphis (Go State!), made their first Final Four appearance. They came up short, but hey, gotta start somewhere, right?
  • 1985: The Danny Manning Show - This time, the Tigers were led by the legendary Danny Manning, a man who could seemingly make a shot from anywhere on the court. They made it all the way to the championship game, but fell to Villanova ( Wildcats forever living rent-free in Memphis fans' heads).
  • 2008: The Derrick Rose Era - The Tigers were a force of nature with Derrick Rose at the helm. They stomped their way through the tournament, even beating the mighty UCLA Bruins. Unfortunately, they fell to Kansas in the National Championship game. (Though some fans still argue a travel call stole their victory... ahem).

So Close, Yet So Far: A Tradition of Excellence (Almost)

Three Final Fours is nothing to scoff at, but Memphis fans are a hungry bunch. They're still waiting for that elusive National Championship trophy. Here's hoping the future holds a title run that will leave Tiger Nation roaring with delight!

How to Channel Your Inner Memphis Tiger

Feeling inspired by the Tigers' March Madness history? Here are some quick tips to unleash your inner Memphis fan:

  • Sport your Blue and Gray with pride.
  • Learn the fight song and belt it out at every opportunity. (Just a heads up, it's a real earworm).
  • Practice your roar. You gotta be ready to cheer on the Tigers!
