Why Did Mendecees Go To Jail?

September 2024 · 5 minute read

Mendecees Harris, a name familiar to fans of the reality television series “Love & Hip Hop: New York,” has faced legal troubles that led to his incarceration. His journey through the criminal justice system has been a topic of interest and concern for viewers and followers of the show. This article delves into the reasons behind Mendecees Harris’s imprisonment, providing a comprehensive understanding of the events that led to his jail time.

Background: Who is Mendecees Harris?

Mendecees Harris is a reality TV star, music manager, and entrepreneur. He gained fame through his appearances on VH1’s “Love & Hip Hop: New York,” where his personal life and relationship with Yandy Smith, a fellow cast member and his now-wife, were prominently featured. Despite his success in the entertainment industry, Harris’s past activities outside of the spotlight have led to significant legal issues.

The Charges: Drug Trafficking Accusations

In 2015, Mendecees Harris pleaded guilty to drug trafficking charges. The charges stemmed from his involvement in a drug trafficking operation that transported cocaine and heroin into the Rochester region of New York State between 2005 and 2012. The following points outline the specifics of his criminal activities:

  • Harris was accused of playing a significant role in a drug trafficking ring.
  • The operation involved the distribution of large quantities of cocaine and heroin.
  • He admitted to his involvement in the trafficking of these illegal substances.

The Plea Deal and Sentencing

As part of a plea deal, Mendecees Harris accepted responsibility for his actions and agreed to forfeit earnings from the drug operation, including his TV show income. His sentencing reflected the severity of the crimes:

  • Harris was sentenced to eight years in federal prison.
  • He was also ordered to forfeit $171,000 from his TV earnings and a luxury car.
  • The plea deal was seen as a way to reduce potential sentencing, which could have been significantly higher.

Impact on His Family

The incarceration of Mendecees Harris had a profound impact on his family, particularly on his wife Yandy Smith and their children. The family’s struggles were documented on “Love & Hip Hop,” showcasing the emotional toll of his absence and the challenges of maintaining family ties during his imprisonment.

Community Reaction and Support

The news of Harris’s sentencing was met with mixed reactions from the public and his fan base. While some expressed disappointment and criticism, others showed support, emphasizing his willingness to accept responsibility and his efforts to turn his life around.

Legal Representation and Defense

Harris’s legal team worked to negotiate the plea deal and present their client in the best possible light, emphasizing his role as a father and community member. They argued for leniency based on his personal growth and the positive changes he had made in his life.

Time Served and Release

Mendecees Harris served his time in a federal correctional facility. His behavior and participation in various programs while incarcerated contributed to his early release. In January 2020, after serving approximately four years of his eight-year sentence, Harris was released from prison, a moment that was celebrated by his family and fans.

Life After Incarceration

Upon his release, Mendecees Harris has been focused on rebuilding his life and career. He has returned to his role on “Love & Hip Hop” and has been involved in various business ventures. Additionally, he has spoken publicly about his experiences, aiming to use his story as a cautionary tale to help others avoid similar pitfalls.

Lessons Learned and Moving Forward

Harris’s journey through the criminal justice system has been a learning experience for him and those who follow his story. He has expressed remorse for his actions and has dedicated himself to being a better person and a positive influence in his community.

Public Perception and Media Coverage

The media coverage of Mendecees Harris’s legal troubles has been extensive, with many outlets reporting on his sentencing and subsequent release. The public’s perception of Harris has been shaped by this coverage, as well as by his own efforts to share his story and make amends.

Continued Advocacy and Outreach

Since his release, Mendecees Harris has been involved in advocacy work, particularly focusing on youth and those at risk of falling into criminal activities. He has participated in speaking engagements and community events, using his platform to promote positive change.

Conclusion

Mendecees Harris’s incarceration was the result of his involvement in a drug trafficking operation, a serious offense that led to significant legal consequences. His journey through the criminal justice system has been marked by challenges, personal growth, and ultimately, redemption. Harris’s story serves as a reminder of the impact of one’s choices and the possibility of turning one’s life around after facing adversity.
