Why Must People Talk From Angle of Poverty? Josh2funny Educates Man Who Criticised His Visit to VP O

September 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Popular comedian Josh2funny and some of his colleagues recently visited the vice president of the country Prof. Osinbajo
  • The gesture however did not go down well with Nigerians who think that the skit makers might end up doing campaigns after collecting money
  • A man specifically noted that Josh and his people collected envelopes of money from Osinbajo and the comedian did not hesitate to school him

Quite a number of Nigerians are angry at a group of popular skit makers in the country after they visited the vice president, Yemi Osinbajo in Abuja.

Josh2funny was one of them, and he took to Instagram to highlight the outcome of the special visitation.

Despite the fact that he touched on how they tabled societal issues at the meeting, a man disclosed that the skit makers would most likely end up doing campaigns for the next election because they have collected envelopes.
