Natalie Imbruglia on turning 40

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Picture: Natalie Imbruglia for Red's July issue

Natalie Imbruglia is the latest star to speak out against Hollywood's ageism. 

Speaking to Red for its July issue, she said: "I wanted people to know I am really proud to be 40. You know, when you are in Hollywood and you say your age they shush you? I think that’s disgusting... I’m fine at the moment. Ask me in five years, I might be having a meltdown." 

The actress joins the debate fresh after 37-year-old Maggie Gyllenhaal's claims in an interview with The Wrap that she was told she was too old to play the love interest of a 55-year-old man. 

Imbruglia also speaks about her desire to have children in the piece, saying: "I definitely want children. Would I have one on my own? I would. I am not at that point yet but I am not opposed to the idea. I’ve got time. It’s a bit tricky that a lot of my friends are on to their third child... that is tough because we all know it’s like a members’ club." 

The July issue of Red is on newsstands tomorrow. To read the full interview click here.
