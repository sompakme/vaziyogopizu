Can a broken timing chain ruin the engine?

In the worst-case scenario, a broken timing belt or chain can quickly and entirely destroy a car’s engine. That’s because most of today’s power plants are what’s known as “interference” engines. If the belt or chain breaks, the valves don’t move and will be smashed by the piston head.
