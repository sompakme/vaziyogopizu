Gigi Hadid Hair Color Evolution - Gigi Hadid Blonde Hair

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Blonde hair has many nuances. For proof, look no further than Gigi Hadid who has been low-key changing up her hair color since she burst onto the scene in 2014. From the bright golden blonde indicative of her Californian roots to the edgy nude dye job she sported at the Met Gala, here's every stage of Hadid's personal blonde ambition tour.

1.Golden Blonde, 2014

Hadid's most peroxide-happy iteration of blonde, she complemented her rosy glow with ultra-fine, mega-watt highlights with golden undertones. 

2.Beach-y Balayage, 2015

The supermodel eventually went for a rootier, more dimensional look with a mix of chunky, hand-painted bright highlights and lowlights meant to mimic her natural color.

3.Sombré Blonde, 2016

In 2016, Hadid shifted to a subtle, grow-out-friendly gradient look with micro balayage highlights to ensure a flawless transition from dark to light.

4.Dip-Dye Blonde, 2016

Hadid temporarily added a little synthetic color to the mix by giving her sombré a turquoise dip-dye treatment.

5.Dark Dirty Blonde, 2016

Later that year, Hadid went even darker with a medium brown base and a slew of golden brown babylights painted around the face, as well as the mids to ends. 

6.Bronde, 2016

Towards the end of 2016, Hadid went just shy of brunette with a gorgeous monochrome "bronde" shade.

7.Champagne Blonde, 2017

For the Fall 2017 shows, Hadid kept her mane monochrome, but lightened up ever so slightly with a cool champagne hue.

8.Nude Blonde, 2017

Always one to commit to a look, Hadid not only lightened her brows to a "whisper," but dyed her hair a neutral "nude" beige to match her gown at the 2017 Met Gala.
