Tom Cruise returns as Ethan Hunt in first trailer for Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read

    For the fifth time in nearly 20 years, Tom Cruise is back as Ethan Hunt - and now he's taking on his most impossible mission to date. Set to a darker version of The Fugee's classic, the first trailer for is a brilliant and classic mix of Mission: Impossible style action, high stakes drama, and laughter.

    SCROLL DOWN FOR VIDEO

    The fifth film in the hugely popular franchises sees Ethan and his team, including William Brandt (Jeremy Renner) and Benji Dunn (Simon Pegg) attempting to finally eradicate The Syndicate, a secret and deadly organisation who will stop at nothing to destroy the IMF.

    Alec Baldwin stars as the Head of the CIA who asks: "How come the CIA has never discovered any intel regarding The Syndicate?"

    Jeremy's William replies: "Do you want the polite answer, or the truth" - suggesting that things are not all that they seem in even the most trusted of organisations.

    Simon Pegg returns for his third film as IMF technician Benji, helping to provide the smarts as well as the comic relief, as Ethan zips around the world by motorbike, car - and as the iconictheme music plays over the final thirty seconds - an Army aircraft.

    There's a good chance the UK premiere of may be a star-studded affair, as Tom is good friends with many British stars - including former England football captain David Beckham and film director Guy Ritchie.

    Tom and David had plenty of time to catch up in 2014 as Tom filmed much of in London. The two were seen having a chilled drink together in June in West London's trendy Notting Hill, sitting at a corner table and sharing a bottle of red wine while David also sipped on a pint of ale.

    Tom and Guy also joined David birthday celebrations earlier in March, with David sharing a photo of them together on Facebook, telling fans: "Here's a pic of me with my dear friends Guy Ritchie and Tom Cruise from my Haig Club London dinner this past Sunday. #HaigClubLondon".
