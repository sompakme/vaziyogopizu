A couple of mischievous Nigerians went down memory lane to unearth a post singer Ayo Balogun, aka Wizkid, made for Nollywood actress-turned-politician Tonto Dikeh.

Legit.ng reported that the musician's arch-rival, David Adeleke, aka Davido, trended online over a similar incident involving the movie star.

The only difference was that Wizkid's post was made a year (2010) before Davido's own (2011).

In the dug-out tweet, the Made in Lagos star confessed his love for the light-skinned actress and noted how she was the only one that mattered to him.
